The Signature Styling Experience is The Style Workshop's most comprehensive offering — a fully managed design process that takes a home from brief to beautiful, with Emily Webster leading every decision along the way.

This package is designed for clients who want the result but not the process: the perfectly resolved home without the hours of research, the indecision at the point of purchase, and the coordination of tradespeople and suppliers. TSW manages all of it, keeping the client informed and involved at the decisions that matter while handling everything else.

The process follows TSW's four-stage methodology: Discovery, where the brief is established and the home is thoroughly understood; Concept Design, where the palette, material language, spatial plan and furniture direction are developed; Refinement, where the concept is worked through with the client until it is exactly right; and Delivery and Support, where procurement is coordinated, trades are briefed, and the home is brought to its finished state.

The $3,950 package fee covers the design work. Procurement of furniture, finishes, and other items is additional and is managed with full transparency — TSW charges a 15% procurement margin, disclosed upfront, on items sourced through the trade. Clients are always shown costs before commitments are made.

Who it is for

The Signature Styling Experience is for clients with the appetite for a genuinely complete result. It suits renovations that are approaching the furnishing and finishing stage, homes that have been purchased and need a comprehensive design approach before moving in, and situations where the client values their time and wants a trusted designer managing the outcome rather than making every decision themselves.

It is worth noting that this is a premium, boutique service. Emily works with a limited number of Signature clients at any time to ensure the level of attention each project deserves. Enquiries are welcome at any stage — even early in the renovation process.
