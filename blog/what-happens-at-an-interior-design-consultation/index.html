What Happens at an Interior Design Consultation?

Many people know they want a more considered home but feel uncertain about where to begin with a designer. What does the first meeting actually involve? What will you receive at the end of it? And is it enough to make a real difference? The Style Workshop Initial Consultation is designed to answer all of this, and to give you something tangible rather than just a pleasant conversation.

Add your consultation or interior image here

What the consultation includes

The consultation begins with a 90-minute site visit. Emily comes to your home, walks through the spaces you want to address, and listens carefully to how you use them, what is and is not working, and what you are hoping to achieve. There is no agenda to push you toward a larger project. The visit is genuinely exploratory, and many clients find it clarifying in ways they did not expect.

During the visit, Emily will cover:

  • The spaces you want to change, and how they connect to the rest of the home
  • How you use the home day to day, and what each space needs to do
  • What is already working and should be preserved
  • Your sense of style, preferences, and priorities
  • Budget considerations and realistic expectations
  • Timing and any constraints relevant to the project

What you receive afterwards

Within three business days, you receive a written recommendations document. This is not a generic checklist. It is a considered, specific set of suggestions for your home, covering furniture, finishes, colour, layout, lighting, and any other areas discussed during the visit.

Alongside the written recommendations, you will receive a set of visual references: curated imagery that gives you a feel for the direction being proposed. These are practical references that communicate finish, proportion, texture, and tone, rather than decorative mood boards.

Together, the written document and visual references give you a clear picture of where the space could go, and enough direction to take action, whether that means working with TSW on the next stage or moving forward independently.

Who the Initial Consultation suits

The consultation works well for homeowners at the beginning of their thinking, for those considering a renovation but not yet certain of scope, and for anyone who wants professional input on a specific space without committing to a larger engagement.

It is also the natural starting point for clients who go on to engage TSW for a Room Edit, Design Edit, or Signature Styling Experience. Having already spent time in your home and understood your priorities, the design process moves more quickly and with greater confidence from the first session.

What it costs

The Initial Consultation is $695, inclusive of GST. This covers the 90-minute site visit, the written recommendations, and the visual references, all delivered within three business days of the visit. Travel within a 30-minute radius of Mosman is included.
