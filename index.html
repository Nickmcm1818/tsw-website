Our Services

Two ways to transform
your home

Whether you're undertaking a full renovation or looking to bring greater cohesion and beauty to an existing space, The Style Workshop offers a complete and considered service — guiding you step by step, and making the process simple and enjoyable.

01
Interior Design

A comprehensive design service for renovations, new builds and significant whole-home projects. We work alongside your existing team or connect you with our trusted network of skilled trades and craftspeople to ensure every detail is delivered smoothly.

  • Spatial planning and review of floor plans
  • Preparation of detailed joinery drawings and full documentation
  • Colour, finishes and material palette development
  • Coordination with trades, suppliers and consultants
  • Furniture, lighting and soft furnishing selection
  • End-to-end project management from concept to completion
02
Interior Decoration & Styling

A refined decoration service for clients who want their space to feel more beautiful, cohesive and personally considered — without a full structural renovation. We love working with pieces you already own, blending them with vintage and one-of-a-kind finds.

  • Furniture (new and vintage), lighting and soft furnishing selection and procurement
  • Custom upholstery and window treatment design
  • Colour consultation and material palette development
  • Art styling and decorative object curation
  • Coordination of delivery, installation and final styling of your home

About Us

Design that begins
with listening

Founded in 2009 by Emily Webster, The Style Workshop is a boutique interior design studio based on Sydney's Lower North Shore. Our work is guided by enduring principles — quality, authenticity and comfort. We believe great design should not only look beautiful, but feel effortless to live in.


We love bringing homes to life through carefully considered furniture, textiles, finishes and thoughtfully layered lighting. We also enjoy working with pieces our clients already own, blending them with carefully sourced vintage and one-of-a-kind items to create spaces that feel personal, collected and unique.


As a full-service studio, we manage every stage of the design journey — from the initial concept through to the final styling. Our process is collaborative, transparent and considered, allowing clients to enjoy watching their vision come together without unnecessary complexity.
